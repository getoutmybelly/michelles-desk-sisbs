Michelle's Desk
Insurance Paperwork Tool

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Michelle's Desk ("the App," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our insurance workflow tool located at getoutmybelly.github.io/michelles-desk-sisbs.

1. What This App Does

Michelle's Desk is a private internal tool used by licensed insurance agents at Illinois Senior Benefit Services. It connects to Google Sheets and Google Calendar to help manage client records, generate paperwork, and schedule appointments.

2. Information We Access

When you sign in with Google, the App requests access to the following:

  • Google Sheets — to read and update client records stored in a private spreadsheet owned by the user
  • Google Calendar — to create appointment and follow-up reminders on the user's behalf

The App does not access any other Google services, files, emails, or personal data beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Information

Data accessed through Google APIs is used solely to provide the features of the App:

  • Reading client records from your Google Sheet to display in the tool
  • Writing updates back to your Google Sheet when you mark items as sent or add notes
  • Creating calendar events when you schedule appointments or follow-ups

We do not sell, share, transfer, or use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose outside of the core functionality described above.

4. Data Storage

Michelle's Desk does not store your data on any external server. All client data remains in your Google account. The only data stored locally is your Google authentication token, which is kept in your browser's session storage and expires automatically.

Commission statement history is stored in your browser's local storage on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google APIs — for authentication, Sheets access, and Calendar integration (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic Claude API — used to read handwritten lead cards and parse commission statements. Images and documents are sent to Anthropic's API for processing and are not stored after the response is returned
  • Cloudflare Workers — used as a secure proxy for API requests. No user data is logged or stored

6. Who Has Access

This App is a private internal tool. Access is restricted to authorized users of Illinois Senior Benefit Services. The App is not publicly marketed or made available to the general public.

7. Data Security

All communications between the App and Google APIs are encrypted using HTTPS. Your Google credentials are never stored by the App — authentication is handled entirely by Google's OAuth 2.0 system.

8. Your Rights

You may revoke the App's access to your Google account at any time by visiting myaccount.google.com/permissions and removing Michelle's Desk from the list of connected apps.

9. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by licensed insurance professionals only and is not directed at children under the age of 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
